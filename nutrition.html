Nutrition Basics

Understanding macronutrients is essential for improving performance, recovery, and overall health.

Protein

Protein helps build and repair muscle tissue and is important for recovery after exercise.

Sources: Chicken, fish, eggs, Greek yogurt, beans.

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are the body's main source of energy and help fuel workouts and daily activities.

Sources: Rice, oats, potatoes, fruits, whole grains.

Healthy Fats

Healthy fats support hormone production, brain function, and overall health.

Sources: Nuts, avocado, olive oil, salmon.

Pre & Post Workout Nutrition

Pre-Workout Nutrition

Eat a balanced meal containing carbohydrates and protein 1–2 hours before training.

  • Banana and peanut butter
  • Oats with fruit
  • Wholemeal toast

Post-Workout Nutrition

Refuel with protein and carbohydrates to support recovery and muscle growth.

  • Protein shake
  • Chicken and rice
  • Greek yogurt with fruit

Hydration Tips

Drink 2–3 litres of water daily. Increase your intake during intense workouts and hot weather.

  • Carry a water bottle throughout the day.
  • Drink before, during and after exercise.
  • Monitor hydration by checking urine colour.
